# Antero Resources (antero-resources)
Antero Resources is an independent oil and natural gas company engaged in the exploration, development, and production of natural gas, NGLs, and oil properties in the Appalachian Basin. It is one of the largest natural gas producers in the United States with operations in the Marcellus and Utica Shale formations.
